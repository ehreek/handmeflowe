How it all started

The company, Hand Me Flowers was founded in 2016 by Crystal Ong with the business purpose of selling flowers online. The company has sold flowers for different occasions such as college and university graduations, Valentines’ day, and wedding. It all started with a bouquet of flowers and today according to trends not only the company is selling flower bouquets, they’re also selling flower boxes, hand bouquet and flower door gift.

 

Besides that, the company uses a portion of the earnings to buy necessities for non-profit organizations and to contribute to society. And by far they have collaborated with different organisations to contribute to conduct workshops for the unfortunate and also visited some places to donate some of their necessities.
